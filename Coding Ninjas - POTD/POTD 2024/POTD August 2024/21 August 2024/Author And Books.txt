Author And Books

Problem statement
You are given a certain format of books and their authors.

For example -

NameOfAuthor1 Book1 Book2

NameOfAuthor2 Book1

Your task is to print the given information in the following manner:

1. NameOfAuthor1

    A. Book1

    B. Book2

2. NameOfAuthor2

    A. Book1
You are given a 2D array of strings ‘S’ where each vector is the information about the i-th author, 
the first index of this vector is the ‘NameOfAuthor’ and the other indexes are the ‘Books’ written by the author. You are supposed to print the Authors and their books in the above mentioned format.
